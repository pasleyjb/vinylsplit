# VinylSplit Vision

## Drag & Drop Automated Music Organization

VinylSplit exists to make music preservation effortless.

Our mission is to transform a single long-form recording into a beautifully organized music library with as little user interaction as possible.

Whether the recording comes from:

* Vinyl records
* Cassette tapes
* Reel-to-reel tapes
* Live recordings
* Radio broadcasts
* Digital recordings

...the experience should always be the same.

**Drop your recording.**

VinylSplit automatically:

* Identifies the album
* Detects track boundaries
* Retrieves metadata
* Downloads album artwork
* Splits the recording into individual tracks
* Organizes the output into a clean music library

The user should never need to understand fingerprints, metadata services, or audio processing.

Those details belong inside VinylSplit.

## Principles

### Simplicity

If a feature makes the application more complicated without providing meaningful value, it does not belong.

### Privacy

Music belongs to the user.

Recordings should remain on the user's computer whenever possible.

No accounts should be required.

No subscriptions should be required.

### Open Source

VinylSplit is built to give back to the open-source community that inspired it.

### Quality

Correctness is more important than speed.

Reliability is more important than feature count.

### Accessibility

Music preservation should be available to everyone, regardless of technical experience.

## North Star

**Drag & Drop Automated Music Organization**

Every design decision should move VinylSplit closer to that goal.
